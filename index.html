PeerPilot

The reviewer recommender engine for modern editorial teams. Instantly surface the most relevant reviewers for any manuscript — based on the full content of the abstract and past publishing history.

Why PeerPilot?

  • Instant reviewer suggestions based on abstract content — no keyword tagging needed
  • Identifies relevant reviewers from all authors who’ve published in your journal
  • Finds new potential reviewers — not just your usual editorial circle
  • Reduces reviewer fatigue by expanding the qualified pool intelligently
  • Speeds up the peer review process by giving you high-quality suggestions immediately
  • Supports fairness and transparency through repeatable, content-based logic
